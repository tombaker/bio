Thomas Baker is an independent Semantic Web consultant currently helping
to build the Global Agricultural Concept Scheme (GACS).  His background
is in social sciences, with a PhD at Stanford University.  He has taught
at AIT in Bangkok and Sungkyunkwan University in Seoul, and he has
worked in Berlin, Washington, and Bonn, where he currently resides.  He
co-chaired the W3C working group that published Simple Knowledge
Organization System (SKOS), the technical basis for GACS, and has served
for fifteen years in the Directorate of the Dublin Core Metadata
Initiative.
